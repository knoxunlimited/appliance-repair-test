Signs You Need Appliance Repair

Appliance repair becomes necessary when performance drops signal internal failures. Alabama Appliance Repair technicians spot these signs during initial inspections.

Refrigerator Not Cooling

Refrigerator not cooling points to compressor strain or evaporator coil frost. Dirty condenser coils block airflow, raising temperatures above 40¬∞F. Low refrigerant from leaks causes uneven cooling. Ignore this, and food spoils while energy bills spike 30%.

Washer Won't Drain

Washer won't drain indicates pump clogs or failed drain hose. Lint, socks, or debris jam the impeller. Water remains in the tub, halting cycles. This leads to mold growth and motor overload.

Dryer Makes Loud Noises

Dryer noises like thumping signal worn drum rollers or loose belts. Rollers seize after 5-8 years of use. Belts fray from heat exposure. Continued runs damage the blower wheel.

Oven Won't Heat Evenly

Oven won't heat evenly stems from faulty bake elements or igniter issues. Elements crack after 3-5 years. Weak igniters fail to open gas valves fully. Uneven baking ruins meals.

Dishwasher Leaks Water

Dishwasher leaks trace to door seal gaps or pump gasket wear. Seals harden over time. Overflow sensors trip if unaddressed. Water damages floors and cabinets.
